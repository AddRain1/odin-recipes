Katsu Curry

Description

Katsu curry is just a variation of Japanese curry with a chicken cutlet on top. I used a store-bought block of Japanese curry roux which is commonly used in Japanese households. Chicken Cutlet (Japanese version of chicken schnitzel) brings the Japanese curry up to the next level. Itâ€™s so delicious and filling.

Ingredients

  • House Vermont Curry
  • Chicken Breast
  • Rice
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Boil the vegetables
  2. Add curry blocks
  3. Cook the chicken and slice it
  4. Plate together and serve
